Open Data Launched July 7, 2014

Governments and organizations worldwide are using ArcGIS Open Data to enable their communities to discover, explore, and download authoritative data from the source.

Since launching, over 100 organizations around the world have joined Open Data, publishing 391 public sites. These orgnaizations include over 99 cities, 43 counties, and 35 states.

Together they have shared more than 13,000 datasets with the community.
